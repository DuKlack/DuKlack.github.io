Benefits Of Vegan Diet

Vegan diet provides multiple nutritional benefits to the human body such as heart health that lowers the risk of cardiovascular disease. It can also help in weight management due to its high content in minerals and nutrition. Vegan diets also lower the risk of chronic diseases like type 2 diabetes and certain cancers. Lastly, veganism can help the environment in a positive way by lowering animal slaughtering and conserving natural resources.

Our Sustainability Practices

At Semplice Verde, working sustainably is our top focus. Wherever possible, we buy ingredients locally which helps cut carbon emissions and assist local farms. We pay attention first and foremost to organic produce, so our meals do not include toxic pesticides or GMOs. In our kitchen, we minimize waste by composting scraps and using eco-friendly packaging for takeout orders. Even our furniture and decor are chosen with sustainability in mind as they are made of reclaimed wood and use energy-efficient light bulbs.
