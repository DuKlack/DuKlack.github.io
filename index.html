Who we are

We are the Semplice Verde family from Palisades High School, providing you with authentic and delicious Italian cuisine since 2025. We created this restaurant to meet the demand for unique vegan options in the Charlotte area, where the Palisades community provided many skilled chefs dedicated to creating nutritious and tasty meals.This year the Technology Student Association requested the development of a new vegan restaurant in the charlotte area. After a long hour of planning, preparation, and design, Semplice Verde was launched, serving you with delightful and healthy vegan dishes.
