Why Vegan?

Vegan food refers to plant-based foods. Our mission is to bring healthy, tasty vegan food to our customersâ€™ tables. Vegan food reduces the risk of chronic diseases, lowers cholesterol, and improves blood pressure. In addition, we also hope to promote sustainability by reducing carbon footprints and supporting local farmers.

Who Are We?

We are the Semplice Verde family from Palisades High School, serving you authentic, tasty Italian food since 2025. We opened this restaurant because we recognize the need for unique vegan foods in the Charlotte area, and the Palisades community is filled with talented chefs who strive to provide nutritious, delightful food. Last year, opportunity presented itself when well-known food enthusiast group Technology Student Association requested a new vegan restaurant be built in their area. After meticulous trial and error, planning, and design, Semplice Verde was opened to the public, providing tasty vegan food with an Italian twist.
